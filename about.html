What we are
Cyberus is an independent security research project based in Greece — built by Ailina S. & Michael C.
What we're not
We're not a company. We're researchers, builders, and learners who work on problems we find interesting.
What we focus on
Cloud security, security analysis, AI in cybersecurity and security tool development.
Our philosophy
Everything we build is open and lives on GitHub. Good security knowledge should be accessible to everyone.

Our Principles

What drives us

🔓
Open Source First
Everything we build is public on GitHub. No paywalls, no gating. Security tools should be accessible to everyone.
📝
Document Everything
We write up our process, our failures, and our findings. How we got there is as valuable as what we built.
🧪
Lab-Tested
Nothing goes on the blog that hasn't been tested in a real or lab environment. We don't post theory as fact.
